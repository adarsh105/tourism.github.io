Contests

Chardham Contest

To give a new dimension to the world’s ancient hiking trail, comprising Shri Yamunotri, Shri Gangotri, Shri Kedarnath and Shri Badrinath shrine, located in the state, Uttarakhand Tourism Development Board (UTDB) is organizing ‘Chardham Hike Narrative’ competition. Participate in this holy and adventurous competition by submitting your narrative of this ancient pilgrimage, including the description of the routes and the mythological/religious/spiritual significance of the stopovers (Padhaavs and Chattis) along with proper documentation.

Contest Details

  • The contest begins at 10:00 am on 8th May 2021 and ends when the divine doors are closed in 2021 (the “entry period”).
  • The contest is open for online submissions only, through the contest page available on www.uttarakhandtourism.in/contests. Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses.

Prize

  • 1st Prize: Rs 1,00,000.00
  • 2nd Prize: Rs. 51,000.00
  • 3rd Prize: Rs 21,000.00
  • 4th Prize: Rs 10,000 for blind participants
  • 5th Prize: Rs 10,000 for physically challenged participants

Eligibility

The competition is open to residents of India aged 18 years and above. The employees of UTDB or their close relatives and anyone otherwise related with the organisation or the judging committee is not permitted to enter the contest.

How to Participate

  • The entire registration process would be online and the intending participants are required to submit their full details by filling the form which is available on the website of UTDB – www.uttarakhandtourism.in/contests
  • The Contestants would have to submit all the details about their on-foot ways of Char Dham along with the evidence depicting the significance, religious or spiritual (or any other), of the places they encounter enroute.
  • Preference would be given to the contestants who will provide photographs and videos of the region along with the mapping. The contest begins at 10:00 am on 8th May 2021 and ends when the divine doors are closed in 2021 (the “entry period”).
  • A detailed presentation to be submitted along with attachments which can be in .jpg, jpeg, .png, .ppt, .pptx, .docx, .pdf format and must not exceed One gigabyte (1 GB) of file size with the links of the videos.
